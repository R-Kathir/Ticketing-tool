Clarifications

Common
Inquiries.

Everything you need to know about migrating your support team, omnichannel routing, and pricing.

How hard is it to migrate from legacy tools?

We built a zero-friction migration engine. Your historical tickets, customer profiles, and existing macros port over from platforms like Zendesk or Intercom in minutes, with zero data loss.

Does Stackly support omnichannel routing?

Yes. Email, live chat, social media DMs, and SMS are all funneled into a single, unified agent workspace. We eliminate context-switching so your team can focus on the customer, not the platform.

How exactly does the AI triage work?

Our system automatically categorizes incoming tickets, analyzes user sentiment, and drafts suggested responses before an agent even opens the thread. It handles the repetitive work so humans can handle the empathy.

What kind of analytics do managers get?

While we optimize heavily for the agent experience, managers get clean, real-time dashboards tracking first-response times (FRT), resolution rates, and CSAT scores, without the bloated, confusing reporting modules of legacy tools.
